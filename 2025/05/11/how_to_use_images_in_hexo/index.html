How to Use Images in Hexo

Posted on 2025-05-11 Edited on 2025-05-12 Views:

Adding images to your blog posts makes your content more engaging, visual, and easier to understand. In this tutorial, you’ll learn the best way to manage images in Hexo.

1. Enable Post Asset Folder

Make sure the following setting is enabled in your root _config.yml:

post_asset_folder: true
